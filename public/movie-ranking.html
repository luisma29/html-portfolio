The Best Movies! (according to Luis)

My top 3 movies of the last 3 years.

Top Gun: Maverick

You know how almost every action movie review says:

"Get ready for edge-of-your-seat action!" when it's pretty much a lie?

Well, Top Gun Maverick will LITERALLY have you on the edge of your seat!!!
The graphics, the acting, the plot (and plot twists) just make this movie so enjoyable...
I was just as excited watching it the second time around as I was when in the movie theater!

Oppenheimer

Sir Christopher Nolan strikes again. This time, he is bringing the story of J. Robert Oppenheimer to the big screen. This is a 3-hour masterpiece that really gives you a fly-on-the-wall perspective of the whole Manhattan project. This is a movie for real film buffs and deep thinkers.

Guillermo del Toro's Pinocchio

A completely unexpected (for me at least) reimagining of the classic Disney tale. This movie came out around the same time a live-actino Disney remake of Pinnochio did and del Toro's film blows it out of the water!
